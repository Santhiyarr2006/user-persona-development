## Project Overview

This project focuses on creating a User Persona for a Hospital Appointment App. The persona represents a typical user and helps designers understand user goals, needs, behaviors, frustrations, and expectations.

User Persona Development is an important part of the UI/UX design process because it helps create user-centered solutions and improves the overall healthcare experience.

## User Persona

### Anitha Iyer

* **Age:** 42 Years
* **Occupation:** Homemaker
* **Location:** Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu
* **Education:** Bachelor's Degree
* **Family:** Married, 2 Children
* **Tech Comfort:** Moderate
* **Device Used:** Android Smartphone

### About Anitha

Anitha is a busy homemaker who values her time. She prefers quick and reliable solutions for her healthcare needs. She faces challenges in getting timely appointments and wants a hassle-free experience from booking to consultation.

---

## Goals

* Book doctor appointments quickly
* Avoid long waiting times at hospitals
* Get reminders for appointments
* Access digital reports and prescriptions
* Easy reschedule or cancellation

---

## Frustrations

* Long waiting time for appointments
* Need to call hospital repeatedly
* Crowded hospitals and unclear queues
* Paper prescriptions get misplaced
* No reminders, forget appointments

---

## Needs

* Simple and quick appointment booking
* Real-time doctor availability
* Appointment reminders and notifications
* Easy access to medical reports
* Clean and easy-to-use interface

---

## Behavior & Preferences

| Activity                | Percentage |
| ----------------------- | ---------- |
| Uses Mobile Apps        | 80%        |
| Prefers In-App Payments | 60%        |
| Looks for Fast Service  | 90%        |
| Values Reminders        | 85%        |
| Checks Reviews          | 70%        |

---

## User Journey – Booking an Appointment

1. Open App
2. Search Doctor
3. Select Date & Time
4. Confirm & Pay
5. Get Reminder
6. Visit & Consult

---

## Pain Points

* Hard to find the right doctor quickly
* Appointment slots not updated in real-time
* Long waiting time even after booking
* Difficult to manage multiple appointments
* No easy way to access old reports

---

## Ideal Features

* Quick doctor search with filters
* Real-time availability and instant booking
* Secure digital payment
* Smart reminders and notifications
* Digital reports and prescription storage

---

## Personality Traits

* Busy Schedule
* Health Conscious
* Tech Savvy

---

## User Quote

## Conclusion

This User Persona Development project provides valuable insights into the goals, needs, behaviors, and challenges of hospital appointment users. Understanding these factors helps designers create more effective and user-friendly healthcare applications. The findings from this persona can guide future UI/UX decisions and contribute to building a seamless appointment booking experience.
